# Stone Run
<img width="350" height="155" alt="card" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/67d12570-8ea0-4f70-991f-5309548d0afd" /><br>

**Stone Run** is a tiny racing game for the Playdate console.

Use the crank to control your car and avoid stones on the road.

Collect coins and keep driving as far as you can.

Your car accelerates every second. Try to beat your high score! <br><br>
